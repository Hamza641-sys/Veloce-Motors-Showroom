Our Legacy

Home / About
Established 2011

A Vision of Pure Velocity

Veloce Motors was born from a singular, uncompromising vision: to assemble the absolute pinnacle of automotive engineering under one roof, providing a bespoke purchasing experience for the world's most discerning collectors and driving purists.

What started as a boutique luxury lounge in Las Vegas has evolved into a global network. We don't merely sell automobiles; we broker mechanical art. Every vehicle on our floor undergoes a strict 180-point verification audit, handled exclusively by our Formula 1 division mechanics.

F.A.Q.

Home / FAQ
How do I purchase a vehicle?

Click "Reserve Now" on any vehicle in our Fleet Gallery to submit a reservation request. Our sales executive will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your allocation, discuss payment terms, and arrange a private showroom appointment or test drive.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we provide fully insured, climate-controlled enclosed delivery to major ports and residences globally. Customs clearing, sea containers, and air transport bookings are managed end-to-end by our logistics division.

Can you source off-market vehicles?

Sourcing limited-edition supercars and classic hypercars is our core specialty. Through our global dealer network and collector relationships, we can frequently procure models not publicly listed for sale.

What inspections do your vehicles undergo?

Every car added to our inventory goes through a comprehensive 180-point mechanical, structural, and history check. Our factory-trained mechanics verify engine diagnostics, chassis integrity, and original parts documentation.

What custom tuning options are available?

Our custom design studio handles full vehicle modifications. This includes custom exhaust fabrications, ECU remaps, high-grade suspension conversions, full carbon aerodynamic paneling, and completely custom leather retrims.
